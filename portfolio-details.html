Case Study: Office Deep Clean

How Zuraya Spotless restored hygiene and safety for a busy office through targeted deep cleaning and sanitization.

Deep Clean 2025

Office Deep Clean — Midtown Towers

Zuraya Spotless completed a full deep clean and sanitization for a 10,000 sq ft office, reducing allergens and improving employee comfort and workplace safety.

Project Narrative

The client requested a deep clean after a busy season. Our team created a phased plan: initial assessment, targeted deep-clean of high-traffic zones, carpet and upholstery treatment, and a final sanitization pass.

We coordinated with building management to minimize disruption and completed the job within two days with measurable improvements in air quality and employee feedback.

Our Solution

We followed a structured cleaning protocol combining assessment, targeted treatments, and final sanitation to deliver measurable hygiene improvements.

01

Assessment & Planning

Conducted a walkthrough to identify high-traffic zones, sensitive areas, and prioritized cleaning tasks to minimize disruption.

02

Targeted Treatment

Applied carpet extraction, steam cleaning, and targeted stain removal using eco-friendly products where appropriate.

03

Final Sanitization & Handover

Completed a disinfectant pass on high-touch surfaces, verified air-quality improvements, and provided the client with a checklist and recommendations.
