Privacy

Your privacy matters to Zuraya Spotless. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Last updated: December 15, 2024

Information We Collect

We collect information needed to provide cleaning services, communicate with you, and process payments when applicable.

Personal Information

  • Contact details (name, email, phone)
  • Service address and scheduling preferences
  • Payment information when you make a purchase (processed securely via third-party providers)
  • Optional notes or access instructions you provide

Usage Data

We may collect analytics data about how visitors use our website (pages visited, device information) to improve our site and services.

How We Use Your Information

We use personal information to schedule and deliver services, communicate with you, manage billing (if applicable), and improve our offerings.

Primary Uses

  1. Provide and manage cleaning services you request
  2. Respond to inquiries and send confirmations or receipts
  3. Improve our services through feedback and analytics
  4. Comply with legal obligations when required

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only when necessary to provide our services or when required by law.

We Never Sell Your Data

Information is only shared with trusted third parties (payment processors, scheduling platforms) and never sold for marketing lists.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure, including secure storage and limited access controls.

Encryption

Sensitive data is transmitted and stored using industry-standard encryption where applicable.

Access Controls

Only authorized staff have access to customer information necessary to perform their duties.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete personal information we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below.

Right to Access

Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

Right to Rectification

Ask us to correct inaccuracies or update your information.

Right to Erasure

Request deletion of your personal data where applicable.

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to operate the website, analyze usage, and improve user experience. You can control cookie preferences through your browser.

Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Required for site functionality Session
Analytics Help us understand site usage Up to 2 years
Marketing Optional cookies for third-party features Up to 1 year

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we make significant changes we will post a notice and update the "Last updated" date above.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy or wish to exercise your data rights, please contact us.
